The Goal

By using genetically engineered e.coli, Urban Tundra strives to remediate toxic Martian soil and generate useful byproducts.

The Future

By innovating e.coli to remediate toxic Martian soil and create useful byproducts, Urban Tundra will be increasing the potential of extraterrestial colonization.

Why?

Urban Tundra strives to turn scientific dreams of yesterday into realities of tomorrow. Through our research, we will take steps to making terraforming a reality.
